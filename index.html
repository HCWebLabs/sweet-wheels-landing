Our Sweet Story

Sweet Wheels was born from a simple dream: to share the joy of homemade desserts with our community. What started in a small kitchen has grown into Knoxville's favorite dessert truck!

We believe that life's sweetest moments deserve equally sweet treats. That's why we use only the finest ingredients, traditional baking methods, and a whole lot of heart in everything we create.

From our family to yours, thank you for letting us be part of your celebrations, everyday moments, and special occasions.
